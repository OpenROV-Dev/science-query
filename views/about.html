What we do.

OpenROV builds low-cost, open-source ROVs to make underwater exploration much more affordable and accessible to the public. The goal of Project OpenScience is to allow OpenROV users to contribute to the citizen science movement by collecting real scientific data with their ROVs that scientists can use to monitor the changing water column.

How to contribute.

Users may add data they collect around the world with their ROVs through the cockpit.
